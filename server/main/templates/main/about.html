Real Impact on Enviroment

Our goal is to promote sustainability in the automotive industry by adopting eco-friendly practices, cutting carbon emissions, minimizing waste, and encouraging the use of green technologies among mechanics and customers.

Why be Eco-Friendly?

Being eco-friendly makes sense because it saves money, keeps us healthy, and ensures we have resources for the future.

How do we contribute:

1

Opting for LED lights, cuts down the use of electricity by around 80% compared to Incandescent or fluorescent bulbs.

2

Rechargeable batteries help cut down costs, reduce waste and they can be used hundreds of times. Also having minimal environmental impact.

3

Integration of solar panels for the workstation, not only powers all the basic functions as well as recharge tools and work vehicles.

4

Recycle metal scraps, oil filters, batteries, tires, or other materials that are no longer needed, and sell them to a scrap yard or a recycling center.

5

Offer virtual consultations and diagnostics to reduce the need to travel and carbon emissions.
