Everything you need to know about break pads:

Brake pad replacement intervals can vary depending on driving habits, vehicle type, and environmental conditions. However, a general recommendation is to inspect your brake pads every 12,000 to 15,000 miles.

Our Recomendation:

Check Maintenance Schedule Check your schedule in your Auto Care Hub profile.

Thickness If the brake pad thickness is less than 3 millimeters, it's time for replacement.

Squealing or Grinding Noises High-pitched squealing or grinding noises when braking can indicate that the brake pads are worn down and need replacement.

Reduced Stopping Power If your vehicle takes longer to come to a complete stop or if you notice a decrease in braking effectiveness.
