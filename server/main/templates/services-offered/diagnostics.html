Diagnostics Services

It's generally recommended to perform diagnostic scans on a car under the following circumstances:

Check Engine Light Illumination If the check engine light comes on, it's advisable to do a diagnostic scan to identify the issue.

Regular Maintenance Including diagnostic scans as part of routine maintenance can help catch potential problems early, preventing more significant issues down the line.

Before Big Trips Before going on a long trip, it's wise to perform a diagnostic scan to ensure that the vehicle is in good conditions for the trip.

After Repairs or Modifications Following any repairs or modifications to the vehicle, conducting a diagnostic scan can confirm that everything is functioning as intended.

Performance Issues If the car is experiencing performance issues such as rough idling, hesitation, or low fuel efficiency, a diagnostic scan can help point out the the issue.

Preventive Measures Some drivers choose to do periodic diagnostic scans as a preventive measure to catch any potential issues before they escalate.
